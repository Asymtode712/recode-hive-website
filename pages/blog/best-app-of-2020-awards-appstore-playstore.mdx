#### App Awards Insights 2020

#### Sleep Stories

Play store rating: **4.4/5 Stars** (1.5k Reviews)  
Downloads: **+10 Million**  
Available on: **Android**

This is not just the first time Google rating has the best app award, in 2018 and 2019 it has able to secure the featuring from Google. Personally, I have used this application for more than 2 years, and it’s very effective. It also helps you to analyze [Cognitive Behavioral Therapy](https://www.mayoclinic.org/tests-procedures/cognitive-behavioral-therapy/about/pac-20384610) which helps you to end the day effectively and balance the stress load. Also one more amazing feature I would like to point out in this app is the UI design. It’s so elegant and materalistic.

*   Helps to [track the sleep](https://www.hopkinsmedicine.org/health/wellness-and-prevention/do-sleep-trackers-really-work)
*   Interactive UI
*   In-app Mentoring and coaching features.
*   In-app Courses and Meditation Features.

#### Koo

Play store rating: **4.7/5 Stars** (36k Reviews)  
Downloads: **+10 Million**  
Available on: **Android**

It also lets the people follow and read the feed and message or post in the form of Text/Audio. Also like other platforms, the users will have profiles. If you are looking for some stress relief then this app can really help you to manage it.

#### Key Features

*   Create Koo
*   Share and view profiles
*   Follow people & Trending Hashtags.
