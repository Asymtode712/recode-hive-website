#### What’s is this [Google Health](https://health.google/) Studies app?

Google Health studies app basically a survey app that helps future studies in the health care sector, you might remember the health care project [Sundar Pichai](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sundar_Pichai) has put forward in [Google IO 2018](https://www.mobihealthnews.com/content/google-io-2018-highlights-ai-health-projects). The application is not available in the Indian market yet. This is pretty similar to the app launched by Apple last year. [Read more](https://www.engadget.com/2019-11-14-apple-research-app-health-studies.html) about that here.

#### What all features this app comes with?

Once you complete the particular survey then you will be getting access to the findings and conclusion on that particular topic. Google just not confined the use case to some particular studies, there will be different options to opt for. Google also lets the user **track the data** you have given. One important thing to highlight here is Google is completely transparent ton the data you contribute, which is mentioned in their official google blog.

![Google Health App](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/Blue-Sweet-Tooth-Blog-Banner-8-1024x576.jpg)

You can find a glimpse of what is google up to in the Health care field from the [Google health research website.](https://health.google/health-research/) The app will let you know **how many surveys you have participated** in and how many people in total the data got collected your monthly contribution etc. All you need to do is select a **survey from the home screen**, give your input, and click on the contribute button. This lets Android users directly contributing to Google health research projects.

**Is Google Health Research Available in IOS?**

No, Google has not released the app on IOS yet, its available in Play store.

**How Google Health research app helps?**

It helps in a more detailed understanding of virus-like covid 19 and other respiratory related disease and research perspective.

**Do Apple has similar app like Google Health research?**

Yes, Apple has launched a similar app last year for both iPhone and iwatch.
