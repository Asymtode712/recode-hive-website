### GETTING STARTED

Google Analytics helps to understand the traffic a Web Analysis tool from Google. In detail, Google has launched in 2005, so why this is so special? This tool helps you with a detailed understanding of your customer and USP. So as a WordPress user this gonna really help you to easily analyze your traffic from the Dashboard.

#### PREREQUISITES

*   Basics of Google Analytics
*   WordPress Installation

**Step 1:** Search Google Analytics and go to analytics.google.com

![Google Analytics Google Search](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/Screenshot-2020-05-16-at-3.42.47-PM.png)

**Step 2:** You will be now in Google Analytics home page, and can see an option to Sign up. Click on that.

![Google Analytics Home page](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/Screenshot-2020-05-16-at-3.43.18-PM-1024x574.png)

**Step 3:** Now you need to give your account name, it can be anything. So here for example I gave business name. so you can give yours.

![Google Analytics create account](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/Screenshot-2020-05-16-at-3.43.29-PM-1024x497.png)

**Step 3:** It will ask you for the marketing details, check the boxes you needed, and click on next.

![Google Analytics](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/Screenshot-2020-05-16-at-3.43.35-PM.png)

**Step 4:** Click and accept the agreement and proceed with next.

![Google Analytics Agreement](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/Screenshot-2020-05-16-at-3.44.28-PM-1024x559.png)

**Step 5:** Now you will be redirected to the Dashboard and one which I marked is the Tracking ID and you will need this to Analyse your website traffic via WordPress.

![](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/Screenshot-2020-05-16-at-3.44.44-PM-1024x432.png)

**Step 6:** A quick view to Dashboard, we will go deep into each on another post, as of now once you set up Google Analytics you will be showing empty later once you come back after integration you can see your metrics.

![Google Analytics home](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/Screenshot-2020-05-16-at-3.59.03-PM-1024x449.png)

**Step 7:** Go to Google Analytics and Install GA Google Analytics, Install and Activate it.

![Google Analytics plugin install](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/Screenshot-2020-05-16-at-3.56.34-PM-1024x366.png)

**Step 8:** Go to your WordPress Dashboard, and go to Plugin settings.

![Google Analytics plugin](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/Screenshot-2020-05-16-at-3.58.01-PM.png)

Step 9: Paste your Google Analytics ID over there. That’s all you need to do to set up Google Analytics, no code paste in your HTML page is no more needed.

![Google Analytics woordpress](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/Screenshot-2020-05-16-at-3.58.17-PM.png)
