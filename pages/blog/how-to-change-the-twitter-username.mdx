How to change the Twitter username
==================================

Tags: tech,Twitter

**Author:** Sanjay KV

**Date:** Mon Dec 21 2020

Having a good username helps to represent your self on the social media platform , especially when comes to twitter the fancy name doesn’t work much or enough traffic. This post will help you to change the twitter username you have one currently.

**Step 1:** Log in to your Twitter Account with your username and password. Click on the **settings and privacy option.** You can find this option on the left of the screen.

![](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/image-2.png)

**Step 2:** Go to Your account on the new screen as shown below, then click on the Account and information. Twitter may **prompt** you to enter the **Username and password again** to edit information further as a 2nd layer of security. Please enter the information as needed.

![](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/image-3-1024x456.png)

You might need to enter the account password then you will see the below screen.

**Step 2:** Click on the **username**, where you can see the old username there, once you click Twitter will let you **edit the username**. Make sure you follow the guidelines of Username. Twitter also provides suggestions for the new username as you enter the blank box. Once you did click on the save button.

![](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/image-4-1024x327.png)

**Is there any limit in changing Twitter username?**

Officially there is no limit on changing twitter username, You can change it several times.

**What is the maximum length Twitter username can be?**

15 Characters is the maximum limit of Twitter username. Make it short always to easy read.

**How to change Twitter Display Name?**

Step 1: Click on Settings and Privacy, then go to accounts.  
Step 2: Click on Account information then change the username
