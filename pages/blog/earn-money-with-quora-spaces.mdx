Earn Money with Quora Spaces
============================

Tags: Monetisation,Quora,Quora spaces

**Author:** Sanjay KV

**Date:** Fri Aug 06 2021

Quora spaces that were in beta testing for a while have been released to the public. So what are these Quora spaces? This is a community-like platform, can compare to the Facebook group and pages. Let’s look at how to make earn money with **Quora spaces.**

Already there is a **Quora partner program** that exists, if you get **100k views normally** on answers then you will get an invitation to join the partner program. Quora space is different you can apply right now to get the account.

[Apply for Quora Spaces](https://www.quora.com/monetization)

**Step 1:** Click the apply for Quora spaces by clicking on the Apply for Quora Spaces.

![](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/08/image-28-1024x478.png)

**Step 2:** Once you click on get started, Enter your location and click on submit. It just collecting the information the payment will be in dollars and **paid via Stripe**. you need to set up a stripe account which will be discussed at the end of the post.

![](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/08/image-29.png)

**Step 3:** In the next step it will ask you to create the Space. Click on creating a space and proceed.

![](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/08/image-30.png)

**Step 4:** It will ask you to enter the name and brief description of what your group/community is all about. Enter the required, remember this can be changed further in the Quora space settings.

![](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/08/image-32-1024x511.png)

**Step 5:** The remaining 2 steps are optional to invite your friends by importing contacts from the Google account associated with quora. Feel Free to skip the process.

![](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/08/image-33.png)

**Step 6:** Now here is the important step of enabling the Monetization part of the Quora Spaces. Click on the Enable monetization.

![](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/08/image-36.png)

**Step 7:** Here is the option to enable space monetization. Like how you see on the Medium partner program.

![](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/08/image-37.png)

**Step 8:** Make sure to click on enable now button.

![](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/08/image-38.png)

Most answers and posts are qualified for adaptation. Exemptions incorporate substance that is set apart as a grown-up, is advanced, or whose topic isn’t allowed under Quora profit strategy. [Quora earnings policy](https://help.quora.com/hc/en-us/articles/360044138031).

![](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/08/image-27.png)

  
Quora+ revenue sharing Earn money from views on the content you reserve for Quora+ subscribers, who pay $5.00/mo or $50.00/yr for access to content from participating Spaces and creators.
