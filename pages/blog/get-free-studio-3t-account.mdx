Get Free Studio 3T Account
==========================

Tags: Bigdata,Data Science,Studio 3t

**Author:** Sanjay KV

**Date:** Fri Aug 06 2021

The previous posts covered the installation of the Studio 3T account and how to configure it, sadly Studio 3T provides only 30 days Free trial. But for students can get it for a **free one-year Licence** version if eligible.

**Note:** Students need to provide verification & institution should be listed on the platform to be eligible for this.

Studio 3T Tutorials covered so far?

[How to start with Studio 3T and Installation Guide](https://recodehive.com/studio-3t-tutorial-installation/)

[How to configure Studio 3T & import the files](https://recodehive.com/configure-studio-3t-tutorial/)

[Apply Here](https://studio3t.com/discount/education/)

**Step 1:** Head to the Studio 3T Education Discount portal, Link is given above. As mentioned above Studio 3T offers a one-year Free license. Here are the following details you need to give while applying.

*   Name and Instiution details.
*   Instituion name
*   Link to the Syllabus
*   Proof of Enrollment

![](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/07/image-113-1024x534.png)

**Step 2:** Once you apply you will be getting a mail in a day or two with an approved license and claim link. Click on the link on the email to claim the license.

![](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/07/image-114-1024x377.png)

**Step 3:** Once you click on the link on email the similar website will appear, all you need is to click on is the claim license.

![](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/07/image-115.png)

**Step 4:** The above thing may be redirected to log in to Studio 3T, Make sure you signup with the Student Email ID.

![](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/07/image-116.png)

**Step 5:** Once you signed up you will be getting a similar screen to this. Copy the Licence key from the My Licence tab. This will require verifying the Software installed.

![](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/07/image-119-1024x552.png)

**Step 6:** Open the Studio 3T Software Installed, Click on the Help Menu and click on My License. Enter the license in the pop-up window appearing.

![](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/07/image-120.png)

License Is not Working on Studio 3T
-----------------------------------

**Step 7:** you may get the issue that the license you enter is not getting validated. All you need to do is to click on But I do have a license.

![](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/07/image-122.png)

**Step 8:** Click on the highlighted option as shown below.

![](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/07/image-123.png)

**Step 9:** Enter your Name, Student Email ID, Paste the license key and click on okay. The license will be validated.

![](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/07/image-124.png)

There may be cases where this is not working if it is still showing not have a license, connect a VPN it will work.
