**Step 1: Use Google search**
-----------------------------

The first step to unlocking the potential of LinkedIn to find the right job is to use Google search. This will help you identify people who currently work at your target company. You can use keywords such as [linkedin.com](https://t.co/wlcfde0xQy) “<company\_name>” resume <role>

For Example: If you want to see resumes of people working as Software Engineers at Google – **[linkedin.com](https://t.co/wlcfde0xQy) “Google” resume Software Engineer**.

![](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/12/Untitled-design-56-1024x256.png)

This should bring up a list of results that include LinkedIn profiles of the current employees of Google. Once you have identified the people working at your target company, you can then proceed to the next step.

**Step 2: Finding their resume on LinkedIn**
--------------------------------------------

Once you have found the company’s LinkedIn page, you can then search for the resumes of individuals working at the company. To do this, you would have to navigate to the profile of each individual working at the company.

Once on the profile, you can then check the featured section or the bio of the individual for a link to their resume. If a link to their resume is available, you can then download or view the resume to get a better understanding of the individual’s skills and experience needed for your desired role.

**Step 3: Connect with them on LinkedIn**
-----------------------------------------

Once you’re done with building your resume with the referred resume, Consider connecting with them on LinkedIn will allow you to build a relationship, learn more about the job, and potentially even get an interview.

When connecting with potential matches, it’s always best to include a personalized message to introduce yourself and explain why you’re interested in the job. This will help to make a good impression and ensure that your message stands out from the rest.

![](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/12/2-1-1024x576.png)

**Step 4:** Follow up with a message
------------------------------------

Once you’ve connected with the potential match, the next step is to follow up with a message. This can be done through a direct message on LinkedIn or through email.

When sending a message, it’s important to be professional and courteous. You should also include a link to your resume and a brief description of your qualifications and experience. This will help to showcase your skills and make a good impression on the potential employer.

Additionally, you can also ask them for advice, referrals, and even job leads. This can be an invaluable way of getting your resume seen by the right people and getting your foot in the door.

**Step 5: Leverage the power of LinkedIn’s job search feature**
---------------------------------------------------------------

Leveraging the power of LinkedIn’s job search feature, allows you to search for job postings that are relevant to your qualifications and experience. It also allows you to narrow down your search to specific companies. This is a great way to find the right job for you.
