#### GETTING STARTED

Google has rolled out a beta version (Early access) of a paid crowdsourcing application known as [Google Task Mate](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.google.android.apps.nbu.tinytask). Here we will explain how to use it and how to get the most payment out of it. You can directly withdraw the money to your e-wallet account once you reach 10$. There is **2 Category of tasks** once you can sit at the home and do other you have to go out, for example, go to the shop and click pictures.

#### HOW TO USE?

**Step 1:** You have to download the app from the [Google Play store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.google.android.apps.nbu.tinytask). Once the app is downloaded on your phone, Select your email account to login then Select any of the languages currently the app support **Hindi and English.** This setting can be changed later from the app settings later.

Then comes the final task of completing the registration which is to enter the **referral code.** Once you enter the referral code it will take you into the application. If you don’t have a referral code don’t worry soon Google will phase out the new rollout in the **upcoming months**.

![Google Task mate ](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/11/Blue-Sweet-Tooth-Blog-Banner-3-1024x576.jpg)

#### HOW TO EARN MONEY?

**Step 2:** Once you are inside the app you can see a few tasks listed below. You have to complete those tasks and you will get the respective money on the app and once it is **over 10$** you can check out **to your bank in local currency**. How cool is it right?

*   Go to shopfronts and click the picture.
*   Record the written sentence in your own voice.
*   Validate the shop details.
*   Translate the sentence.

#### SIMILAR APP IN MARKET

You can find a similar app in the market like Google Task mate few of them are [Google Opinion rewards](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.google.android.apps.paidtasks) and [Amazon Mechanical Turk](https://www.mturk.com/). Google opinion rewards work in a different way it will ask for surveys and you will get paid for the survey. You can make money **based on the survey** you receive and the frequency of the survey depends upon the region you live in. Also cash out in Google opinion rewards is directly to play store money but in Google Task mate you can check out the money directly to your **e-wallet account**.
