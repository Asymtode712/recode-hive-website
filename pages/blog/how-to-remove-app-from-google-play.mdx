#### GET STARTED

Google Play Store UI changed recently and the procedure to remove the app from the play store has been changed. If you are a developer of an app and want to **delete the app from the Play Store listing**, Google only lets you unpublish the app from the [Play console](https://play.google.com/console).

#### Steps to remove app from play store console

**Step 1:** Go to [https://market.android.com/publish/Home](https://market.android.com/publish/Home) and select your developer account by default users will navigate to the app list of their developer.

![](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/image-2-1024x316.png)

**Step 1a:** After that, select the app you want to Unpublish, In my case, I want to **unpublish** Alan wallpaper so new users can’t download the app from Play Store.

![How to unpublish an app in Google Play Developer Console](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/04/image-163-1024x362.png)

**Step 2:** In addition, the Next task is to move to **Setup** then click on **Advanced settings**. Under t**he App Availability** tab you can see an option to unpublish the app, click on the radio button. Here the app status will be mentioned. Once done click on Save Changes as shown below.

![delete my app from Google Playstore](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/04/image-164-1024x501.png)

**Step 3:** A pop-up window will come asking whether the user wants to really remove the app from the play store. Click on **unpublish.** However, Doing this will unlist the app from the [Playstore](https://play.google.com/store) and the **existing users can use this**, **but no more people can download your app** from Google Play Store. Similarly, click on the confirm button to proceed.

![](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/image-1.png)

Once it is unpublished you can go to the main menu and see the app status changed to **Removed by you.**

**Note:** As mentioned above, developers can only unpublish the app from the Play store, As the existing user already installed apk on their system they will be able to use it even after unpublishing.

![](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/04/image-165-1024x427.png)

How to remove App permanently from Google Play console List
===========================================================

There is another way to permanently delete app listing from google play console, All you need to [fill this application form.](https://support.google.com/googleplay/android-developer/contact/publishing) this will redirect to your play store Google support team and you can explain the details as shown below and **click on submit.**

![deleting app permanently from console list](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/04/image-166-1024x607.png)

So Far you have seen how to remove app listing from Google play console lets look into how to delete the app from Google play.

Time needed: 2 minutes

**How to delete app from google play** Store

1.  **Step 1: Open Google play store on your mobile device**
    
2.  **Step 2: Navigate to My apps & Games**
    
3.  **Step 3: Select your installed apps from the list, click on uninstall.**
    

**Can I republish the unpublished app later?**

Yes, you can republish the app later, if you have not changed any changes to the App apk file then no need for review.

**If I unpublish an app will it remove from all the phones?**

No. People who still use the installed version of the app can continue using whereas new people may not able to download it.

**How to completely remove an app list from Google play console Dashboard?**

It’s not possible to completely remove an app list from the google play console, you can only unpublish the app list stays there.

****How** to remove the rejected app from the **google play console****

yes. You can go to the Play console support page for reference and fill the application.
