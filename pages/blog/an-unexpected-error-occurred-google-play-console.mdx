### GETTING STARTED

Trying to Upload or edit the new details google play console and getting error like Unexpected error, please try again. So Mostly it’s happening because of the beta version and the version you use is obsolete.

![](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/07/Screenshot-2020-07-01-at-4.37.16-PM-1024x270.png)

So Google play store beta version is available from July 2020 [click here to new Play store console](https://play.google.com/apps/publish/), now you will be able to upload or manage your release. Link for the new beta version is given above.

[Google Play console Beta](https://play.google.com/apps/publish/)
