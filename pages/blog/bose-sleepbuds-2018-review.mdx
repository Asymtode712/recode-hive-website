#### What is Bose [Sleepbuds?](https://www.bose.com/en_us/products/wellness/noise_masking_sleepbuds/noise-masking-sleepbuds.html)

Bose Sleepbuds is not like other in ear earphones, it’s designed to do particular task, such as to reduce the sleep deficiency. Which always produce some soothing sound for a better sleep.

![](https://i2.wp.com/recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/02/Capture2.jpg?fit=640%2C310&ssl=1)

#### Features:

1.  Sound design Blocking
2.  Preloaded sound 24hrs
3.  Wireless kit & alarm

![](https://i1.wp.com/recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/02/ip1.jpg?fit=640%2C311&ssl=1)

#### Functionalities:

The main Function of this is to provide soothing sound by cancelling the outer noise and make you a comfortable sleep, The have in pack 3 comfortable tips ( different size ear bud) which fit perfectly in your ear.

#### Storage:

They provide Good storage Feature also, this elegant storage cover also provide 16Hr battery on one time recharge.

#### [Price:](https://www.amazon.com/Bose-785593-0010-Noise-Masking-Sleepbuds/dp/B07CMNMLNK)

Price is bit high when you compare the other device. this cost around 245$ in market, but i would say its actually worth it. Really a great product. Also the company provide 30 day trial usage.

#### How to use:

The Sleepbud is very easy to use, charge your device, once the charging done, download the earbud app from the [Playstore](https://play.google.com/store?hl=en) or [Iphone](https://www.apple.com/in/ios/app-store/) store. After that download the sound from the store which will take 1 hour to download once you done that you are ready to go. Connect the device in your ear and have a nice sleep.

#### Where to buy?

You can buy this product from [Bose online store](https://www.apple.com/in/ios/app-store/) or from [amazon](https://www.amazon.com/Bose-785593-0010-Noise-Masking-Sleepbuds/dp/B07CMNMLNK) or wall mart.
