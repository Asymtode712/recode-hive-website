The launch of these new devices is expected in September or October of 2020, featuring some of the major, specialized updates and new design challenges.

Focusing on the design, it is said that one of the new iPhones coming in 2020 will feature a specific redesign with a **metal frame,** which has a distinctive, **flat look for its frame**, composed of **stainless steel**, which is quite similar to the frame of the iPhone 4 or the iPad Pro. #### FEATURES

Newer camera systems with triple-lens setups will be featured by higher-end iPhones, while the lower-end iPhones will feature dual-lens camera setups. Designs and colors As mentioned before, the new iPhone will feature a stainless steel frame, that would replace the current models having stainless steel frame, and it is also composed of glass in the front as well as the back of the device.

The molding of the metal, which is grooved and injected, in the frame is said to reduce the negative impact of metal shielding on the high-frequency transmission efficiency of the internal antenna and the glass is either tempered or made of **sapphire**.

#### LET’S TALK ABOUT DISPLAY

Speaking of colors, the iPhone model would come in several colors like blue, black, orange, violet, and some colors. Details on displays Speaking of displays, the OLED used across the iPhone lineup by Apple is allowed for a more flexible handset design. **OLED displays** from the company BOE Display, where LCDs are made specifically for Apple iPads and MacBooks are tested by Apple.

Apple has been using OLED displays from the Samsung company in recent OLED iPhones and is expected to continue this, business with Samsung. It is said that Samsung would be supplying around 80 percent of displays used for the upcoming iPhone 12 models. 5.4-inch iPhone 12 – OLED display, **6.1-inch** iPhone 12 Max – OLED display, 6.1-inch iPhone 12 Pro – OLED display from Samsung, **using XDR**. The displays are specialized with improved OLED which is supposed to bring more reality and fluidity.

#### BATTERY LIFE

These new devices are improved with battery life, mainly in speed and efficiency. The iPhones coming in 2020 could have some small gains in battery capacity. The Apple suppliers are working on a specifically designed, custom battery protection module that is around 50 percent **slimmer, smaller, and compact**.

Then the similar components were used in previous iPhones. The main perspective is to free up valuable space on the iPhone.The iPhone 12 models are designed to have a battery life of about **45 to 50hours.**

#### 5G CONNECTIVITY

**Qualcomm** modem chips are utilized instead of Intel chips, for the Apple iPhone 12 models with the aid of **Qualcomm’s 5G technology**. Qualcomm’s first-ever 5G chip is used that provides a wide range of all major frequency bands, network deployments, and different modes of operation. **Sub-6GHz** connectivity is also being used which could be actually disabled in countries that do not offer 5G or have a penetration of 5G that is shallow.

The touch ID and Face ID in the new iPhone 12 models also feature new improved sensors and wider view angle or a wider range of angles to unlock the device.

#### TECHNOLOGY ADVANCEMENT

Apple designers are claiming to ultimately be aiming to remove most of the external ports and buttons on the iPhone for an advanced, efficient, and streamlined device. Apple is trying to improve the wireless charging technology so that it can neglect the charging technology requiring wires.

These aspects could definitely bring an improvement in technology, where it involves making the software more advanced. Apple is said to release an **iPhone without a Lightning port**, probably by 2021.
