Now the question is how to track them all. #### Tool Demo: [Mentions](https://mention.com/en/)

By using the tool you can find **Brand alerts** – People who talk about your brand on social media, **Industry mentions** – People who talk about you on social media. Engaging with this audience will drive you more traffic to the website, Its equally important like keyword Monitoring, apart from the search you can drive more traffic through Social Media Channels.

**Step 1:** Go to Mentions social media tracker online tool, Click on the Login button above.

![Best social Media Montioring Tool](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/04/Social-Media-Tracker-1024x521.png)

**Step 2** Click on 14 Days Trail, so you can try the software and purchase if you have good use. **The Good thing is no credit card required for the free trial.** Once you signup accepts their terms and conditions. You have to give the below information to create the account

1\. Provide Full Name
2. Job Position
3. Phone Number
4. Company Name
5. Company Size
6. Agency otpion
7. Industry
8. Select the plan 

![Signup to Mentions a Social Media Montoring tools](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/04/image-84-1024x576.png)

**Step 3:** Once you Signed up Enter the brand or product you want to monitor in the social media. Then click on Next step to proceed further.

![Setting up brand to Monitor on Social Media Montoring tool](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/04/image-86-1024x439.png)

**Step 4:** Give the social media username of the brand you want to monitor like below.

![Give social Mention on social Media](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/04/image-87-1024x462.png)

**Step 5:** This step is **optional** you can enter the brand you want to find the competency, its simillar to AB testing in SEO. Try to add the 4 brand you think its your competitor and click on Get started to proceed.

![Competitor Analysis](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/04/image-88-1024x437.png)

**Step 6** Next step is again optional which will ask you to add all social media account to one place much similar to Social media Manger, I already using **Publer** for this [social media manager](https://recodehive.com/best-social-media-scheduler-in-2020/), skipped the option here.

**Step 7:** There are additional features like MFA(Multi factor authenication) it will be more useful if you are working as agency and reduce the hacking of your account. Now you are ready to go to dashboard.

![UI demo of Mentions of Social Media Montoring tools](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/04/image-89-1024x331.png)
