#### GETTING STARTED

Are you worried about fewer interactions on your Instagram profile? Then no more worries here you go with new story apps, which can help you with Instagram story creations. It’s not just a template to create the stories it also helps you to target the right audience with your content.

Unfold is my favorite, its not so unique from other apps, what made me like this app is the wide variety template the app has. There are 200+ templates available in the app and which keeps updating every day. Cool Isn’t it? The template you can also use for other social media as well.

*   Template & Colleges.
*   Font and Text Editors.
*   Filters and Effects.
*   Unfold the Camera.
*   Unfold +(Pro Version of the app).

#### PICSART

We all know Instagram has amazing stickers on the list, but if you want to show your creativity I doubt Instagram can fulfill it. I use Picsart instead. It’s like a drag and drops photoshop apps that play with frames and animation, for newbies it might take some time to play with UI but later it’s so smooth. Let me list down the features.

*   Voice over for animations.
*   Layers for High-level animations.
*   Real-time animated selfies.
*   No ads..

#### GBOX

GBox has some different purposes, if you like to edit the photos then this s all in one solution for editing your photograph, Maybe very few people know about the existence of this quality app. Also, the Image splitter tool is really amazing. There has been some lag and less resolution quality photos coming when you edit high-resolution pics, I hope the developer will fix the issue as soon as possible.

*   Color picker- pick a color from any photo.
*   Glitch Effect.
*   Video Splitter.
*   Swipeable Video.

#### GARNY

The purpose of this app is again different, I basically use to organize the Instagram feeds. You can schedule photos and videos in one place. Also, you can connect your Instagram and directly schedule it from the app itself. One limitation I can see is that if you are an influencer or running Instagram multiple account Garny allows only to integrate only 2 accounts into the free version.
