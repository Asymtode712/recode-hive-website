Google IO Event 2021. All you need to know
==========================================

Tags: Event,tech

**Author:** Sanjay KV

**Google IO** the annual developer event where Google unveils all plans and products which gonna come into the market, plus developer exclusive updates and fun activities. This year Google IO is gonna happen on May 18 -20, and the full agenda of the event will be released by Google in Late April 2021.

#### How to register for [Google IO 2021?](https://recodehive.com/events-list/)

The Registration process is lengthy as compared to last year, This time Google has introduced a puzzle to solve for developers, here is the link [https://events.google.com/io/punchcard](https://events.google.com/io/punchcard?lng=en), the punch card includes 5 quiz type questions.

**Step 1:** Go to the website [https://events.google.com/io](https://events.google.com/io), then click on Register to proceed

![](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/04/image-23-1024x289.png)

**Step 2:** You should have a Gmail Account to continue, if you dont have one create your gmail Account now.

![](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/04/image-24-1024x446.png)

**Step 3:** Signup with your Gmail Account done then click on continue on the next screen which will take you to a screen where the user needs to enter personal information.

What all information & consent Google Ask?
\* First Name & Last Name
\* Country of Residence
\* Concent to provide your Gender information for Audience Analysis
\* Race & ethicity Identification
\* Age
\* Company/ Organisation
\* Job Role
\* Product which your company Develop
\* Part of any Google Communities
\* Email ID
\* Your topics of interest
\* What questions you will ask to Sundar pichai if you have chance?
\* Twitter username

![](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/04/image-25-1024x573.png)

**Step 4:** Once you fill all the information above you can agree their terms and conditions, and click on submit button.

![](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/04/image-26-1024x473.png)

Registration completed you will get the option to create a developer badge, Go ahead and click on the developer badge to create one. You will also get a mail like below to complete the developer profile which is a beta web application to track the developer progress on code labs and so on.

![](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/04/image-28-1024x578.png)

![](https://recodehive.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/04/image-30-1024x680.png)

Email confirmation for the event.
